//Data Model Types//

Information Reference Model (L1 & L2)
	Enterprise-wide view of information and data.
	Defined in terms of subject data.
Conceptual Data Model (L3)
	High level view of key entities and concepts in use in the company covering all domains.
	Defined in terms of key entities and relationships.
Logical Data Model (L4)
	Complete view of data for a solution or interface / service.
	Normalised or dimensional depending on solution type.
	Independent of technology or platform.
Physical Data Model (L5)
	Implementation schema for a specific technology / platform.
